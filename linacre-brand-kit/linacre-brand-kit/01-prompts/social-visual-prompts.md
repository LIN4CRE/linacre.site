# Social Visual Prompts — Linacre.site

Use these prompts to create consistent graphics for LinkedIn, X, GitHub, YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, and Bluesky. Paste the **Master Brand Prompt** first.

## 1. LinkedIn Banner

### What this is for
Your LinkedIn banner should instantly communicate full-stack + AI engineering and match the site.

### Prompt

> Create a LinkedIn banner for David Linacre / Linacre.site. Size 1584x396. Visual style: calm retro-modern developer workspace, dark ink background, warm amber glow, subtle hexagon grid, terminal microcopy, small pulse waveform, clean code/card elements. Include readable text: “David Linacre” and “Full-Stack & AI Engineer”. Optional subtext: “Tools · Roadmaps · AI Experiments · Clean Web Systems”. Leave safe space on the lower-left for LinkedIn profile photo overlay. Premium, warm, technical, not cluttered.

### Export

- `linkedin-banner-1584x396.png`
- Keep important text away from edges and profile image zone.

## 6. Social Post Background Template

### What this is for
A reusable background for quotes, tips, build updates, and carousel slides.

### Prompt

> Create a reusable social post background template for Linacre.site. Square 1080x1080. Dark ink background, subtle amber radial glow in one corner, faint hexagon/grid pattern, small terminal label at top, space for a large headline, space for 2–3 bullet points, small Linacre.site wordmark at bottom. Calm, premium, developer-focused, readable, not cluttered.

## 7. Carousel Template

### What this is for
LinkedIn/Instagram carousel posts explaining tools, roadmaps, and lessons.

### Prompt

> Design a 7-slide carousel template for Linacre.site. Theme: practical full-stack and AI engineering tips. Use consistent dark/amber visual system, large readable headings, simple diagrams, terminal-style labels, and minimal code snippets. Slides: 1 cover, 2 problem, 3 key idea, 4 example, 5 tool stack, 6 checklist, 7 CTA. Keep it premium, calm, and easy to scan on mobile.

### Slide structure

1. Cover: strong promise.
2. Context: why it matters.
3. Main concept.
4. Example or mini workflow.
5. Tools/resources.
6. Checklist.
7. CTA: “Save this / explore Linacre.site”.

## 10. Social Visual Rules

Every social graphic should have:

- one clear idea,
- large readable text,
- dark/amber recognition,
- consistent margins,
- a small Linacre.site mark,
- no more than two accent colours,
- a calm premium feel.

If a post needs a long explanation, make it a carousel instead of squeezing everything into one image.
