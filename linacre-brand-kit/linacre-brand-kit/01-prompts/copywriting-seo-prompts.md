## 1. Website Homepage Copy Prompt

### What this is for
Use when rewriting the hero, section intros, CTAs, and footer text.

### Prompt

> Write polished website copy for Linacre.site, a full-stack and AI engineer portfolio. The copy should be clear, calm, practical, and technically credible. It should explain that the site includes a curated toolkit directory, self-taught developer roadmap, AI Lab/sandbox, changelog, and full-stack portfolio. Tone: warm engineering clarity, not corporate, not hype-heavy. Use short headings, concise paragraphs, and direct CTAs. Make it feel useful for self-taught developers and credible for recruiters/collaborators.

### Output requested

- Hero headline options
- Hero subheadline options
- CTA labels
- Toolkit section intro
- AI Lab section intro
- Roadmap section intro
- Changelog intro
- Footer sentence

## 2. SEO Metadata Prompt

### Prompt

> Create SEO metadata for Linacre.site. The site is a full-stack and AI engineer portfolio featuring a curated free developer toolkit, self-taught roadmap, AI sandbox/lab, and changelog. Tone should be practical and premium. Provide title tags under 60 characters, meta descriptions under 155 characters, Open Graph title/description, Twitter card copy, keywords, and structured data suggestions. Avoid keyword stuffing.

### Suggested metadata starter

Title:
> Linacre.site — Full-Stack & AI Engineering Toolkit

Description:
> Tools, roadmaps, AI experiments, and clean web systems from full-stack and AI engineer David Linacre.

## 10. Copy Rules

Good Linacre.site copy should:

- explain the useful thing quickly,
- sound like a real builder,
- make technical ideas approachable,
- avoid overclaiming,
- invite people to explore or save,
- connect back to tools, roadmap, AI Lab, or builds.
