## Master Prompt

You are helping build the brand system for **Linacre.site**, a personal full-stack and AI engineering portfolio created by David Linacre.

The brand identity is: **amber · hexagon · pulse · cyber**.

The brand should feel like a calm, premium, soft retro-modern engineering workspace. It combines late-night coding energy, warm amber terminal light, clean full-stack product design, and a subtle AI-lab atmosphere.

The website currently represents a full-stack and AI engineer, a curated directory of free developer tools, a self-taught engineering roadmap, an AI sandbox/lab, a changelog, and a premium React/TypeScript/Express workspace.

### Desired mood
Calm, clever, useful, warm, technical, self-taught, premium, practical, late-night, quietly futuristic.

### Visual cues
Deep ink backgrounds, warm amber glow, subtle hexagon geometry, soft pulse lines, clean cards, terminal microcopy, code details, glassy panels, retro synth warmth, modern UI restraint.

### Audio cues
Quiet felt piano intro, warm Rhodes chords, subtle 80s synth pads, gentle bass, soft lo-fi/easy-listening drums, tiny digital glitches, tasteful emotional lift, no harsh EDM.

### Typography direction
Modern geometric headings, readable body text, monospace code/terminal accents. Suggested fonts: Space Grotesk, Inter, JetBrains Mono.

### Colour direction
Primary colours: ink black `#070A0F`, deep slate `#0B1220`, amber `#F59E0B`, amber glow `#FFB000`, warm cream `#F5E7C8`. Use cyan `#22D3EE` or violet `#A78BFA` only as rare AI/lab accents.

### Brand voice
Clear, practical, builder-led, calm, technically credible, not corporate, not hype-heavy. Explain useful things simply.

### Avoid
Generic corporate SaaS, harsh cyberpunk, neon overload, gamer style, crypto visuals, fake robot clichés, cold blue AI branding, clutter, hard EDM, cheesy stock music, overused “AI brain” imagery, unreadable low contrast, excessive glow.

### Output quality
Make the result polished enough for a real portfolio, GitHub profile, LinkedIn banner, social post, or website launch. Prioritise clarity, consistency, and recognisability.

## How To Use It

1. Paste the master prompt first.
2. Paste the specific prompt you need.
3. Replace anything in `[square brackets]`.
4. Ask the AI tool for 3–5 variations.
5. Choose the calmest, clearest version.
6. Export into the matching folder inside `02-assets/`.

Use this with image/video tools when possible:

> Avoid aggressive neon, chaotic cyberpunk, gamer logos, complex tiny details, fake robot faces, generic SaaS blue gradients, corporate stock photo style, crypto/web3 clichés, random icons, excessive glow, over-sharpened 3D, childish mascot style, unreadable text, incorrect spelling, watermark, low resolution, cluttered layout, cheap tech poster look.
