# Audio Identity Guide — Linacre.site

## 1. Audio Brand Summary

The Linacre.site sound is:

> quiet soft retro easy-listening with a modern digital pulse.

It should feel like coding late at night with warm amber light, not like a loud tech advert.

## 2. Emotional Target

The listener should feel:

- focused,
- calm,
- curious,
- reassured,
- quietly energised,
- like something clever is being built.

## 3. Instrument Palette

### Primary

- Felt piano
- Rhodes/electric piano
- Soft analog synth pad
- Gentle bass
- Lo-fi/easy-listening drums

### Secondary

- Tiny digital pulse
- Soft glitch tick
- Warm tape noise
- Subtle vinyl texture
- Quiet bell-like accent

## 4. Structure Rule

The requested structure:

```text
Quiet piano intro
→ warm verse build
→ tiny emotional chorus burst
```

The chorus should not become loud. It should feel bigger in the listener's head through harmony, warmth, and lift.

## 5. Tempo and Key

- Tempo: 82–90 BPM
- Key: warm minor, dreamy major, or modal jazz/soul colour
- Feel: relaxed but not sleepy

## 6. Required Audio Assets

### 3-second sting
Use for logo animations and end cards.

### 8-second intro
Use for quick branded social intros.

### 15-second cue
Use for Reels, Shorts, TikTok, LinkedIn demos.

### 30-second loop
Use only if a website visitor actively turns audio on.

### 60-second brand bed
Use for walkthroughs, launch videos, and longer demos.

## 7. Mixing Guidance

- Keep transients soft.
- Avoid harsh high frequencies.
- Let piano/Rhodes carry the signature.
- Keep bass warm and controlled.
- Digital pulses should be small ear-candy, not the main rhythm.
- Master around -16 LUFS for social/web video.
- For background website loop, master quieter around -20 to -24 LUFS.

## 8. Website Use

Website audio must be optional.

Do:

- use a clear play/pause button,
- remember preference with localStorage,
- keep volume low,
- provide mute control,
- avoid autoplay.

Do not:

- autoplay music on page load,
- hide controls,
- use loud stingers on interactions,
- make audio essential to understanding the site.

## 9. Social Use

Use audio to make posts recognisable:

- start with the same piano motif,
- use the same tiny pulse sound,
- end with a warm Rhodes resolve,
- pair logo sting with hex-pulse animation.

## 10. Producer Brief

If hiring a musician/producer, send this:

> I need a soft retro-modern audio identity for Linacre.site, a full-stack and AI engineer portfolio. The brand is amber, hexagon, pulse, cyber — but calm and premium, not harsh cyberpunk. Think felt piano, Rhodes, subtle analog synths, easy-listening/lo-fi drums, gentle bass, tiny digital pulses. Structure should start with quiet piano, build into a warm verse, then bloom into a small emotional chorus lift. No vocals. No EDM drop. No corporate jingle. I need 3s, 8s, 15s, 30s loop, and 60s versions with consistent motif.
